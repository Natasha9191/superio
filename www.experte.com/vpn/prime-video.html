VPNs aren't just for staying anonymous online, they also let you circumvent annoying geo-blocking that prevents you from accessing foreign content on Amazon Prime Video. Of course, streaming services do everything they can to stop this from happening since they've paid handsomely to distribute that content to specific markets.

Good VPNs should help bypass these restrictions and change your virtual location. To evaluate this more closely, we checked which of our sample's providers can unlock US Amazon Prime Video content around the world.

Best VPN for Amazon Prime Video: Our TOP 5

Of the 27 platforms in our comprehensive evaluation, 15 grant access to Amazon Prime Video content from abroad. To compare, 19 allowed access to Netflix, leading us to believe that Amazon is a tougher nut to crack. On the other hand, Disney+ is only accessible by 13 of the services.

How We Tested

We thoroughly tested all 27 VPNs from our sample, comparing their performance with one another across six different categories, five of which were scored. Below, we've summarized these aspects for you:

Installation and Features

A VPN client is your gateway to a VPN network and should be user-friendly. The VPN services that performed best in this area combine ease of use with a large number of features and settings. The number of supported operating systems and devices also played a role in scoring this category.

Server Network

The more, the better: When it comes to server networks, we look favorably upon services with as many servers spread as widely across the world as possible. Server numbers range from less than 100 up to 34,000, while we rated providers present in 60 or more countries as solid.

Performance

We constantly measure our sample using a 1 GB/s connection with a server in Frankfurt (Germany) as part of our VPN speed test. To augment this, we also tested each service for a day, to see how they were for surfing and streaming.

Security and Privacy

When it comes to privacy VPN providers like to promise the moon. We ignore their "No-Logs" promises or guarantees, preferring instead to read between the lines of their privacy policies and read their corporate histories. Another thing we check is external security audits. Finally, we perform VPN leak tests and review each service's encryption standards and VPN protocols.

Customer Support

Support is scored based on two components: direct support and static support. The first should be as responsive, fast, and technically competent as possible, preferably in the form of a live chat. For emails and support tickets, we like to receive responses within 24 hours. Static support should be full-text searchable, clearly arranged, and include guides and FAQs.

Conclusion

If you already take a lot of time selecting a film or series to watch, our advice might make your life harder. But, there's just no other way to watch content outside of what's available in your geographic region. The five services we've listed above are all solid choices for unlocking access to Amazon Prime Video content from abroad.

However, you should take care, since streaming services do as much as they can to prevent VPNs from allowing users to watch geo-blocked content. For this reason, availability can fluctuate, however, our Top 5 are known for their reliability. Comprehensive reviews for all 27 VPN services from our sample can be found in our extensive evaluation.

FAQs

Which VPNs work with Amazon Prime Video?
⊖

Of the 27 providers in our sample, 15 were able to provide access to foreign Amazon Prime Video content. Most of the services that allow users to access Amazon Prime Video work with other streaming platforms like Netflix and Disney+ as well.

What's the best VPN for Amazon Prime Video content?
⊖

NordVPN reigned supreme in our comprehensive evaluation, offering an excellent all-around package and reliable streaming. Behind it, in second and third place came ExpressVPN and Surfshark, both of which provided access to foreign Amazon Prime Video content.

Is it legal to access Amazon Prime Video with a VPN?
⊖

It isn't illegal, even if it clashes with Amazon's terms of use. The company notes that "you may not use any technology or technique to obscure or disguise your location." All the same, you don't have to worry about any legal consequences as we're not aware of cases of Amazon accounts being suspended or locked owing to VPN usage.

Which country has the best Amazon Prime Video catalog?
⊖

This depends on what films or TV shows you're looking for. Unfortunately, no comprehensive list showing what's available where exists. This might be part of Amazon's efforts to limit VPN usage with its Prime Video platform.
