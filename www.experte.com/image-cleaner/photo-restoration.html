Restore Old Photo: Automatically & Free

You have an old photo that has scratches and damages? With our tool you can automatically restore old pictures - directly in the browser and without Photoshop knowledge.

No technical knowledge is required to use it. Just upload the image, mark the unwanted scratches and after a few seconds you will get the restored image. You can repeat this process as many times as you like until the image is completely restored.

How to Use

1. Upload the image you want to restore automatically.

Old picture with scratches and damage

2. Select the scratches that should be removed automatically.

Selected scratches

3. As soon as you release the brush, the selected area is automatically restored. This can take up to 30 seconds, depending on the resolution of the image.

Picture without damages

4. Download the restored image - in full resolution, of course.

FAQ

How to restore old photos automatically?
⊖

Simply upload the image in our tool above and select the scratches and damages you want to remove. Our tool will then automatically calculate how this spot should look like and show you the result after 10 - 30 seconds. You can then download the image without damages in full resolution.

How does the tool work?
⊖

We use a state-of-the-art machine learning model (LaMa), that has been trained to automatically replace unwanted things in photos. The tool itself is based on the open source solution LaMa Cleaner.

What are the limits of the tool?
⊖

You can process any number of images of any size with our tool. However, the larger an image is, the longer it will take to process. The tool can also be used for commercial purposes.
